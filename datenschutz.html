Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 October 2024

1. General Information

Protecting your personal data is very important to us. In this privacy policy, we explain what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your personal data.

2. Responsible Entity

The entity responsible for data processing on this website is:
Muniqo Performante

3. What Data We Collect

We collect personal data when you visit our website, register, fill out a form, or contact us. The types of data we collect include:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number.
  • Technical Data:IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, referrer URL.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website.

4. Purpose of Data Processing

We use your data for various purposes, including:

  • To provide and improve our website.
  • To process inquiries and communicate with you.
  • To fulfill legal obligations.
  • To analyze user behavior and optimize our services.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

The processing of your personal data is based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent under Article 6(1)(a) GDPR (for the use of contact forms and cookies).
  • Performance of a contract under Article 6(1)(b) GDPR (when you use our services).
  • Compliance with legal obligations under Article 6(1)(c) GDPR.
  • Legitimate interests under Article 6(1)(f) GDPR (e.g., for analyzing user behavior).

6. Disclosure of Data to Third Parties

Your personal data will only be shared with third parties if it is necessary to fulfill a contract, you have explicitly consented to it, or if we are legally required to do so. Possible recipients include:

  • IT service providers operating our website.
  • Third-party analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Authorities, where legally required.

7. Cookies

We use cookies to make our website more user-friendly and to collect statistical data on usage. For more information, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to request information about the personal data we hold about you at any time. In addition, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Rectification: You may request that inaccurate data be corrected.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request the deletion of your data unless legal retention obligations apply.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You may request the restriction of data processing under certain conditions.
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to receive your data in a commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You may object to the processing of your data based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw your consent to data processing at any time.

10. Data Security

We employ technical and organizational security measures to protect your data from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Please visit this page regularly to stay informed about updates.
