Cookie Policy

Last updated: 22 October 2024

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Muniqo Performante uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow us to save your preferences, analyze your visit, and provide you with an improved user experience. There are different types of cookies with different functions.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are essential for the operation of our website. They enable you to move around the website and use its basic functions, such as accessing secure areas. Without these cookies, the website cannot function properly.

3.2 Preference Cookies

These cookies allow us to store information that influences the behavior or appearance of the website, such as your preferred language or the region you are in.

3.3 Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use our website, such as which pages you visit most often. This information helps us improve the performance of the website.

3.4 Marketing and Targeting Cookies

These cookies are used to show you relevant advertisements based on your interests and browsing behavior. They help us make ads more effective and ensure you don't see the same ads repeatedly.

4. How Do We Use Cookies?

We use cookies for various purposes:

  • Website Operation: Some cookies are necessary for our website to work and provide you with all the features.
  • Analytics: We use cookies to analyze user behavior and improve our website.
  • Advertising: We and our advertising partners may use cookies to provide you with relevant content and ads.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and block the storage of cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that this may affect the functionality of our website.

Browser Settings: You can disable or delete cookies in your browser settings. The exact procedure depends on the browser.
Changing Consent: You can withdraw your consent to cookies at any time by changing your preferences in the settings of our website.

6. Third-Party Cookies

Some of the cookies we use are set by third parties. This includes analytics providers (like Google Analytics) or advertising services. These third parties have their own cookie policies, and we recommend reviewing those as well.

7. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please check this page regularly to stay informed about updates.
